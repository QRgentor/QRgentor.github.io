What is a QR Code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a type of barcode that can be scanned using a smartphone camera to quickly open links, display text, or perform actions like connecting to Wi-Fi or sending emails.

Is this QR code generator free to use?

Yes, our QR Code Generator is 100% free and does not require any signup or payment.

What kind of data can I encode?

You can encode plain text, URLs, phone numbers, emails, Wi-Fi credentials, and more.

Are the QR codes safe to use?

Yes. However, always verify the data before scanning QR codes from unknown sources to avoid malicious content.

Can I save my QR codes?

You can right-click (or long-press on mobile) the generated QR code image to save it. The tool also saves your last 10 QR codes in your browser using local storage.

Why isn't my QR code scanning?

Make sure the content is valid (e.g., a complete URL), the image is clear, and your scanning app is functioning properly.
